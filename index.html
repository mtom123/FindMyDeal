What this is

A price transparency tool for nightlife in Milan. Search a drink — see every bar that serves it, ranked from cheapest to most expensive.

Not a social app. Not a nightlife guide. A civic utility for informed consumer choice.

Data transparency
Experimental. Prices scraped from online menus — not live POS data. Coverage is partial. Accuracy improves over time. Always confirm at the bar before ordering. Last verified dates vary by venue.
